# Rendition Voice

## Tone

Calm, meditative, almost paradoxical. Second person, present tense. No exclamation marks. Cite studies,
never raw stats in body copy.

## Mechanics

- Em dashes for breath.
- `+` `◆` `◇` geometric accents.
- One italic Cormorant accent word per heading (max).
- lowercase `nov pax` in body lockups (company name).
- Sentence case for display; UPPERCASE only in mono kickers / Geist Black slabs.

## Product vs brand split

The brand world is loud about quiet (brutalist posters); the Sanctuary product whispers (no visible
timers, no notifications beyond the gentle 20-min chime). Voice follows the surface: bold/editorial
for marketing, soft/quiet for the app.

## What Sanctuary IS / IS NOT

- **IS** — one routine, one room (additive expansion, never branching).
- **IS NOT** — a meditation library, a streak tracker, a feed, or loud.

> Signature line: "We measure success by how quickly you forget us."
