Soft fields, never slabs.

Use radial dusk + lilac at 10–18% opacity. The room is lit by atmosphere, not painted.

+ CHAPTER 06 / PRODUCT · SANCTUARY · ONE ROUTINE NPX-PD-BODY
06
+ PRODUCT · SANCTUARY

It runs in the background.

The product app is the eye of the storm — soft orb, calm room, gentle chime. The brand world yells QUIET so the product can whisper it.

Sanctuary is not a library of practices, and not a streak tracker. It is one quiet companion that sits beside your work. You open it, tap Begin, and use your screen.

After twenty minutes, a soft chime arrives and Sanctuary takes over with a short audio-guided meditation. You're asked to close your eyes — the screen darkens on purpose.

  • 01 Home, at rest. A single room. The companion breathes. One Begin button.
  • 02 Focus, silently. No visible countdown — by design. A soft arc grows, that's all.
  • 03 Look away. Dark take-over. Audio orb. "Close your eyes."
  • 04 Settings & future routines. Voice, interval, soundscape. Future cards (wind-down, posture, walking) appear in the same room.
RESTING
Welcome back.
