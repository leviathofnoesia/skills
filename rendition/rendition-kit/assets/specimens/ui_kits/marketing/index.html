+ FAQ · TEN QUESTIONS

Asked, answered.

P. 05 / 06
Is this a meditation app?

Not exactly. Sanctuary holds one routine — a 20-minute screen-rest cycle with a brief audio-guided look-away. No library, no daily quotas, no streak nudges.

Will I see a timer?

No. By design. A soft arc grows quietly in the background, but the timer itself is hidden. You're meant to forget the room is keeping count.

What happens at the chime?

A gentle audio cue arrives. The screen darkens, the companion blooms, and a soft voice asks you to close your eyes. Sixty to ninety seconds, then the room returns.

Can I change the voice or interval?

Yes. Settings hold the voice, the interval, and the soundscape. Defaults are 20 minutes with Maren, on a porcelain hum.

Are there other routines coming?

A few — evening wind-down, posture nudges, walking rests. They will appear as additional cards in the same room, never as branches into a library.

What about data and privacy?

No telemetry beyond what you can see in Settings. Audio is generated locally. Account is optional. We measure success by how quickly you forget us.
