+ Manifesto

The internet asked for your attention.
We are giving it back.

We believe technology can be tender. That a screen can be a place to arrive, not escape. Nov Pax is a small atelier of designers and clinicians making quiet software — products that meter their own use, hold their tongue, and never measure your worth in streaks.

+ Three commitments

What we will always do.

+ 01

Hold the space.

No ads. No algorithmic feed. No engineered urgency. The product opens in stillness and closes the same way — what happens between is yours.

+ 02

Earn the trust.

Every protocol is co-built with practicing clinicians and meditation teachers. Sources are cited. Claims are modest. We would rather under-promise than over-perform.

+ Questions

Things people ask us.

Is this another meditation app?

+

Sanctuary is one practice tool inside Nov Pax. The bigger project is more philosophical: a small studio making consumer software that doesn't trade your attention. Meditation is a starting point, not the destination.

Why no streaks or notifications?

+

Streaks reframe a contemplative practice as a video game. Notifications interrupt the very thing we are trying to give you back. We measure success by depth of session and time off-screen, not return frequency.

How do you handle health data?

+

End-to-end encrypted, stored on-device by default, with opt-in cloud sync that uses zero-knowledge keys we cannot read. We are HIPAA-aligned (not a covered entity), GDPR compliant, and we do not sell, license, or model on your data — period.

Who builds this?

+

A team of nine — designers, two clinical psychologists, an ex-Calm engineer, and a Carmelite monk who keeps us honest. Based in San Francisco and Porto.
