+ 01 / Home · The cozy space

A single, consistent room.

Sanctuary is not a library of practices to choose from. It is one persistent companion — a soft, ambient surface you can open at any moment and feel held.

On the home view: the breathing orb, the current routine (the 20-minute screen-rest cycle), and a quiet status of where you are inside it. Everything else lives in Settings, out of the way.

  • One orb. One routine. One tap to begin.
  • Soft mint dot = a session is running silently in the background
  • No tabs, no library, no leaderboards
  • Customization tucked behind a single gear
