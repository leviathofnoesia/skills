# Rendition Brand — Nov Pax

Nov Pax is the company; Sanctuary is the product. Brand world is loud about quiet; the Sanctuary
product is the soft eye of the storm.

## Brand identity (Nov Pax)

Copy the structure of `preview/brand-identity.html` (bundled in `rendition-kit`; this skill's
`references/brand-identity.md` summarizes it): logo (black + white v3), wordmark, brand gradients
(`--grad-aurora`), philosophy, social. This is the `FOLIO 01 / 11` 11-folio brand kit — distinct from
the 8-plate specimen (`rendition-edition`).

## Logo

`logo-black-v3.png` / `logo-white-v3.png` in source `assets/`; wireframe corner SVGs in `assets/icons/`.
On light use the black mark; on Onyx/Dark use white.

## Brand kit imagery (28 rasters)

All 28 live in `assets/brand/` (this is the ONE skill that bundles the rasters — load-bearing for brand
collateral, never injected into context). Names: `archer-iridescent`, `atomic-poster`, `biomorph-dots`,
`cloud-infinity`, `connected-rooms`, `glass-refraction`, `gradient-maps`, `gradient-poster`,
`kit-black-{art,color,logo,social,typo,website}`, `kit-white-{art,color,logo,social,typo,website}`,
`lissajous`, `logo-lotus-eye`, `mandala-soft`, `novpax-org-glass`, `orb-grid`, `painterly-flora`,
`pixel-paradise`, `spectrum-portal`.

## Art direction

Surrealist brutalism × heavenly vaporwave warmth; halftone moons beside breathing orbs; mega Geist Black
900 titles on chunky duotone plates; crop marks / registration cross / barcode / marquee chrome frame a
single Cormorant italic line. References: Bráulio Amado, Pentagram NY editorial, Hassan Rahim, Ettore
Sottsass, Mubi late-2020s rebrand.

## Anti-patterns

- Never use the retired v1 biolum/magenta/solar spectrum on brand surfaces.
- Nov Pax company name is never changed.
- Bundled specimens hardcode literals for source-fidelity — references, not production patterns.
